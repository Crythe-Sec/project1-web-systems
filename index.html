I gotta say, I was a pretty big fan of the first half of the new Dune movies, I thought it was a pretty good way to adapt the book while still being true to the source material. Granted, there's a lot that's lost without the borderline unhinged paranoia every character has in their inner dialogue, and I feel like that's a big part of the appeal of the original series, especially considering how that bit ramps up to 11 in the later books, but I digress. Overall, really good movie, and I'm pretty hyped for part 2, I hope they pull it off!
